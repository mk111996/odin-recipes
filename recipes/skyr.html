Skyr with Honey and Nuts

A perfect scandinavian dessert!

Skyr is a traditional Icelandic dairy product (kind of a yogurt), pretty high in protein.

Ingredients

  • Skyr
  • Honey
  • Mixed nuts, chopped
  • Berries (optional)

Steps

  1. Put skyr in a bowl.
  2. Top it with some honey (you can mix it in if you like).
  3. Add some chopped nuts of choice.
  4. Serve immediately.
