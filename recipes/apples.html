Salted Caramel Apples

A sweet dessert!

Perfect for autumn and cold evenings, this special treat is one of the most popular desserts in the North.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Heavy cream
  • Salted butter
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Cut the apples, remove the seeds.
  2. Prepare some salted caramel on a saucepan using sugar, water, heavy cream and sea salt.
  3. Pour caramel over apple pieces or cover entire apples in caramel. Using it as a side sauce is also a good idea.
