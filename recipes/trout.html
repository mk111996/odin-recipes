Dill Cured Trout

Another classic, and a delicious way to serve fresh fish!

Cold-cured trout, freshly caught in a scandinavian river.

Ingredients

  • Trout fillets (with skin)
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Juniper berries
  • Crushed black pepper
  • Fresh dill

Steps

  1. Combine salt, sugar, berries and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Cover fish fillets with salt mixture and sprinkle with dill.
  3. Wrap some plastic around the fish and let it cure for at least one day.
  4. Unwrap the fish and remove the excess salt mixture and dill.
  5. Slice thinly and serve with some rye bread.
